# FittedForYou — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 30, 2026**

FittedForYou ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data when you use our mobile application ("App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

- **Email address** — required for account creation and login via Supabase Authentication.
- **Password** — stored as a securely hashed value by Supabase; we never see your plain-text password.
- **Location data** — collected once per session to retrieve your local weather forecast. We do not store your precise location on our servers.
- **Outfit history** — the clothing items you log each day (tops, bottoms, shoes, outerwear, comfort rating, outfit type, and associated weather conditions).
- **Wardrobe inventory** — the clothing items you mark as owned in the Wardrobe tab, including optional color data.
- **App preferences** — notification settings, laundry reminders, and dismissed banners stored locally on your device.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the App:

- To generate personalized daily outfit suggestions based on your wardrobe and local weather.
- To track your outfit streak and weekly outfit recap.
- To send optional push notifications (daily outfit reminders, comfort check-ins, laundry reminders, and weekly recap). You control these in Settings.
- To improve our suggestion algorithm by analyzing patterns in your comfort ratings and outfit history.
- We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.
- We do **not** use your data for targeted advertising.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your account data, outfit history, and wardrobe inventory are stored securely using [Supabase](https://supabase.com) (supabase.com), a third-party backend service. Supabase stores data in data centers within the United States. Each user's data is protected by Row-Level Security policies that prevent any user from accessing another user's records.

App preferences and a local cache of your wardrobe are also stored on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless synced with Supabase.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

- **Supabase** — authentication and database. [Privacy policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Open-Meteo** — free weather API used to fetch your local forecast. No personal data is sent; only your device's latitude and longitude are transmitted. [Privacy policy](https://open-meteo.com/privacy)
- **Google AdMob** — used to display non-personalized ads on the free tier. AdMob may collect device identifiers as permitted by your platform privacy settings. [Privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Expo (by Expo Inc.)** — the platform used to build and deliver the App, including push notification infrastructure. [Privacy policy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account and outfit data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (by contacting us at the email below), we will permanently delete all your personal data from our servers within 30 days. Local device data is removed when you uninstall the App.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

FittedForYou is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.

- Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
- Object to or restrict certain processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **cameron.lettieri@outlook.com**. We will respond within 30 days.
